About

The Syracuse International Film Festival (SIFF) is an annual celebration of independent cinema that showcases diverse films from around the world while fostering cultural exchange and supporting emerging filmmakers. Founded to bring world-class cinema to Central New York, SIFF provides a platform for both established and up-and-coming artists to share their stories with audiences in Syracuse and beyond.
